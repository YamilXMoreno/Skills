Problem Statement:
# Title: `ConditionTimeoutException` doesn't expose the underlying cause of evaluation failures.

## Description:
When a condition fails due to an internal error rather than a true timeout, `ConditionTimeoutException` omits this cause, preventing users from identifying where the failure originated.

Requirements:
- When a condition fails due to an `AssertionError` during its evaluation, and the timeout expires, the resulting `ConditionTimeoutException` should expose that error as its root cause.

- The `AssertionError` exposed as the root cause of `ConditionTimeoutException` should preserve its original type, allowing callers to identify the underlying failure.
